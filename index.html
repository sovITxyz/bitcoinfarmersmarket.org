A Salvadoran circular-economy market

Bitcoin Farmers Market started in El Zonte in July 2023 — born out of the Bitcoin Beach community and run by a small crew of locals and volunteers. Every other Saturday, vendors gather at Club Cocal on the Surf City coast to sell food, coffee, crafts, and more. Bitcoin is the preferred currency, but cash is welcome. Bring a bag, bring a friend, and come spend your sats.

  • ⚡️ Bitcoin preferred

    Lightning, on-chain, NFC — pay how you like. Efectivo aceptado.

  • 🌱 Hyper-local

    Salvadoran farmers, makers, cooks, roasters. Fresh, hand-made, often organic.

  • 🌴 Built by community

    Birthed at Bitcoin Beach with help from the bitcoiners who call El Salvador home.
